Welcome to Janine Mc Daid Counseling

My name is Janine McDaid, and I have my counselling practice in Donegal. I also offer online therapy sessions to accommodate my clients’ needs. I understand that life can become challenging and overwhelming at times. Having a warm and compassionate space to talk things through can make all the difference. If this is what you're seeking, I am here to help. Life often throws us new challenges, whether it's work stress, mental health issues, relationship problems, or family struggles. It's easy to feel out of control and at the mercy of your circumstances. Therapy can help change this dynamic – it allows you to take back control of your life and prioritise your personal growth. I have extensive experience working with a variety of issues, including depression, anxiety, burnout, and relationship struggles. However, you don’t need to be in crisis to benefit from therapy. If you feel the desire to work on yourself and improve your life, therapy can provide that support. If you are ready to take this step, I am here to support you. Learn a little more about me here

Counselling

Life demands flexibility, and my approach to therapy mirrors this necessity. I offer both in-person and online sessions to accommodate your busy schedule and seamlessly integrate therapy into your life. Whether you need long-term or short-term therapy, I tailor my services to your unique needs and goals. If you're interested, I provide a free consultation call to discuss the best approach and medium for you, with the understanding that your needs may evolve over time. Therapy should adapt to life's changes, and my services are designed to do just that.

About

I am a highly trained and experienced integrative person-centred therapist based in Donegal. I have extensive experience working on a variety of challenges, including mental health issues, domestic abuse, and relationship and work-related problems. My approach is to provide an empathetic and supportive environment where you can explore the reasons for seeking therapy and discover your path to wellbeing. Whether you are in crisis or simply wish to improve your life, I am here to help you find a way forward through therapy.
