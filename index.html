Welcome to Janine Mc Daid Counseling

My name is Janine McDaid, and I have my counselling practice in Donegal. I also offer online therapy sessions to accommodate my clients’ needs. I understand that life can become challenging and overwhelming at times. Having a warm and compassionate space to talk things through can make all the difference. If this is what you're seeking, I am here to help.

Life often throws us new challenges, whether it's work stress, mental health issues, relationship problems, or family struggles. It's easy to feel out of control and at the mercy of your circumstances. Therapy can help change this dynamic – it allows you to take back control of your life and prioritise your personal growth.

I have extensive experience working with a variety of issues, including depression, anxiety, burnout, and relationship struggles. However, you don’t need to be in crisis to benefit from therapy. If you feel the desire to work on yourself and improve your life, therapy can provide that support.

If you are ready to take this step, I am here to support you.
